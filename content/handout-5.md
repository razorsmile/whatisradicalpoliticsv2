## Research Your Local Housing Context

To understand your local housing crisis, find out:

- **Average house price:** How many times the local average salary?
- **Average monthly rent (2-bed):** What portion of the average wage?
- **Council housing waiting list:** How many people? What's the average wait time?
- **Rough sleepers:** How many in your town or city?
- **Empty properties:** Are there statistics available?
- **Affordability ratio:** Can you calculate house price to salary ratio?

## Three Strategic Responses

### IN: Policy Solutions

- Rent controls
- Build council housing
- Tenant protections
- Wealth taxes for housing

### AGAINST: Direct Action

- Rent strikes
- Eviction resistance
- Squatting
- Disrupting property capital

### BEYOND: Alternative Models

- Housing cooperatives
- Community Land Trusts
- Mutual aid networks
- Self-build projects
