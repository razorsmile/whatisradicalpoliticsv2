<div class="section-block">

## Mutual Aid Networks

- Direct action unions and solidarity networks
- Community fridges and food banks
- Mental health peer support groups
- Migrant solidarity organisations
- Informal childcare networks

## Self-Organised Spaces

- Radical social centers
- Community-owned pubs and cafes
- Black and minority ethnic community spaces
- Community gardens and urban agriculture
- Squatted social centers (when active)

## Alternative Economies

- Local exchange trading systems (LETS)
- Worker cooperatives
- Credit unions - community-controlled finance
- Time banks - hour-for-hour skill exchange
- Free shops and gift economies

## Knowledge Commons

- Radical film festivals
- Reading groups and study circles
- Free universities and autonomous education
- Radical book clubs and libraries
- Alternative media and community publishing

</div>
