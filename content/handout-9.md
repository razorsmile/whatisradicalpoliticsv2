## Introductory Texts

- **"Anarchism: A Very Short Introduction"** - Colin Ward
- **"Communism: A Very Short Introduction"** - Leslie Holmes  
  *The 'very short introduction' series is a good way to get a quick overview of a topic.*
- **["Feminism is for Everybody"](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1kS76Zch-PMw7-Bc10sZeVCQWAk2KPCuB/view)** - bell hooks
- **["A Decolonial Ecology"](https://consortiumbooks.org/critical-south-series/a-decolonial-ecology-thinking-from-the-caribbean-world/)** - Malcom Ferdinand

## On Strategy

- **[Organisational Strategy Resources](https://www.yourparty.uk/organisational-strategy/)** - Your Party strategy document Nov 2025 (pre-founding conference)
- **["Inventing the Future"](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inventing_the_Future:_Postcapitalism_and_a_World_Without_Work)** - Srnicek & Williams

- **"Brighton: Old Ocean's Bauble"** - Edmund Gilbert
- **[QueenSpark Books](https://queensparkbooks.org.uk/)** - Various oral histories
- **[Brighton Digital History](https://brightondigitalarchives.com/our-collections/)** - Online archive

- **["Research as Resistance"](https://canadianscholars.ca/book/research-as-resistance-2nd-edition/)** - Brown & Strega
- **["Workers' Inquiry and Global Class Struggle"](https://www.jstor.org/stable/j.ctv17ppcfh)** - Edited collection
- **["Militant Research Handbook"](https://citizenmediaseries.org/2015/02/01/the-militant-research-handbook/)** - Precarious Workers Brigade
