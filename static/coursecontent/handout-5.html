HANDOUT 5: Brighton Housing Crisis Facts

The Numbers

  • Average house price: £525,000
  • Average monthly rent (2-bed): £1,800
  • Council housing waiting list: 2,000+
  • Rough sleepers: 150+
  • Empty properties: 1,000+
  • Ratio of house price to average salary: 15:1

Three Strategic Responses

IN: Policy Solutions

  • Rent controls
  • Build council housing
  • Tenant protections
  • Wealth taxes for housing

AGAINST: Direct Action

  • Rent strikes
  • Eviction resistance
  • Squatting
  • Disrupting property capital

BEYOND: Alternative Models

  • Housing cooperatives
  • Community Land Trusts
  • Mutual aid networks
  • Self-build projects
